VIVUM '24 - India's Largest Student-Led Charity Event

Organized to bring together schools from across Bangalore to compete in exciting sporting and cultural events. As Chief Organizer, I oversaw design, social media, and marketing operations as well as sports events. To ensured collaborative efforts from all volunteers, I tried to keep the process engaging by coordinating whole grade dance for opening and closing ceremonies and fashion show as a break.

Zephyr - India's First Student-Led Interschool Mental Wellness Fest

Zephyr helped create a platform for open discussion about mental wellbeing spearheaded by professionals in the fields of counselling. It provided artistic avenues (music, dance, art, and poetry) as competitions for self-expression, promoting wellbeing and expression.

TISB Hacks - India's Largest Student-Led Hackathon

An online hackathon centred around the the theme of technology with humanity at its core through promoting projects for social good, education, healthcare and sport. We hosted professionals in the field to engage with participants in sessions and judge project deliverables and introduced new mini-events: Tech Trivia & Code Wars.

Sahaya Project (NGO)

Dedicated to promoting child welfare, holding community and awareness at the core of our cause. Organizing 6 initiatives till date, we have reached multiple underserved communities to provide opportunities to young individuals for their holistic growth.

AI/ML School Club

As Co-President, I helped foster a collaborative environment to enhance our skills in the application of AI and Machine Learning systems. We aimed toreated a community for innovation and technological exploration. We digitized a live house-point tracker and are working to digitize the school Lost and Found to ease access.
